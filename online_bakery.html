About Us

Nestled in the heart of Jakarta is a family-owned bakery that has been dedicated to crafting the finest pastries and bread with a passion for baking and a commitment to using only the freshest ingredients. From flaky croissants and buttery danishes to crusty baguettes and hearty sourdough loaves, this bakery offers a wide variety of pastries and bread to suit every taste. Be sure to visit when you pass by in Jakarta!
